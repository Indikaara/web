Heritage & Traditions

Every piece from Indikaara carries the legacy of ancient Indian craftsmanship, passed down through generations of skilled artisans.

Centuries of Skill

Indian craftsmanship dates back over 5,000 years, with techniques refined through countless generations. From the intricate weaving patterns of Rajasthan to the brass work of Moradabad, each region has developed its unique artistic identity.

Traditional Techniques

Hand-weaving, metal forging, wood carving, and natural dyeing methods

Regional Expertise

Each state specializes in distinct crafts passed through family lines

Master Artisans

Meet the skilled hands and creative minds behind our collections, preserving ancient arts in the modern world.

Carpet Weavers of Mirzapur

Masters of traditional knotting techniques, creating intricate patterns that have adorned homes for centuries.

Brass Artisans of Moradabad

Skilled metalworkers who transform raw brass into stunning decorative pieces using traditional hammering and engraving techniques.

Wood Carvers of Saharanpur

Expert craftsmen who bring life to wood through intricate carving, creating both functional and decorative masterpieces.

Techniques & Materials

Authentic craftsmanship begins with the finest natural materials and time-honored techniques.

Hand Weaving Traditions

Our rugs and dhurries are created using traditional pit looms and hand-spinning techniques. Cotton and wool are carefully selected, hand-spun into yarn, and dyed using natural colors from plants and minerals.

  • • Materials: 100% organic cotton, pure wool, natural silk
  • • Dyeing: Vegetable dyes, indigo, turmeric, madder root
  • • Techniques: Flat weave, pile knotting, dhurrie weave

Traditional Metalwork

Our brass and metal decorative pieces are crafted using centuries-old techniques of hammering, engraving, and finishing. Each piece is individually shaped and detailed by hand.

  • • Materials: Pure brass, copper, mixed metals
  • • Techniques: Hand hammering, engraving, oxidizing
  • • Finishing: Hand polishing, natural patina, lacquering

Wood Crafting Excellence

From intricate carvings to functional furniture, our wood artisans use sustainably sourced hardwoods and traditional carving tools to create lasting pieces of art.

  • • Woods: Sheesham, mango wood, teak, rosewood
  • • Tools: Traditional chisels, hand planes, carving knives
  • • Finish: Natural oils, wax polishing, hand buffing
