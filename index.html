The Soul of the Craft.

Generations of Skill

Each Indikaara rug is the culmination of knowledge passed down through generations. Our master artisans in rural India use age-old techniques, ensuring every knot is tied with intention and purpose. This isn't just a process; it's a living heritage.

The Finest Materials

We believe a timeless rug begins with timeless materials. We source the highest quality natural fibers, from lustrous silk to durable, hand-spun wool. The richness of our colors comes from traditional, natural dyes that are both vibrant and sustainable.

A Unique Story

No two handcrafted rugs are exactly alike. Each piece carries the subtle variations and unique touch of the weaver who created it. When you bring an Indikaara rug into your home, you are not just acquiring a beautiful object, but a unique story woven in thread.
